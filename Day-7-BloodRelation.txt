 A is B's  daughter , B is C's mother , D is C's brother How D is related to A?

 a) Father b) God father c) Son d) brother


 Soln

 D is related to A as brother.

Explanation:
- B is C's mother. This means B is the female parent of C.
- A is B's daughter. This means A is the female child of B.
- Since B is C's mother and A is B's daughter, that makes A and C siblings.
- D is C's brother. This means D is another sibling of C.

Therefore, D is related to A as a brother because they both share the same mother, which is B.
