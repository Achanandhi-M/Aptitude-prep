4) A Number is dividend by 406 leaves remainder 115, what will be remainder when it will be divided by 29?

Sol
Formula -> dividend=divisor X quotient + remainder
        -> n=406 X x + 115
        -> n=406x+115
n/29=406x+115/29
    = 406x/29 + 115/29
remainder=28

or use another simple Tricks
 -> 406+115/29
 -> remainder is 28
