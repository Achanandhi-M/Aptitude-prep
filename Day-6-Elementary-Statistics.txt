What is the difference between the mean and the median of the given data 5, 9, 8, 15,12, 9,2,19,21 and 11 ?

Soln

Mean= 5+ 9+ 8 + 15+ 12 + 9 + 2+ 19+ 21 +11 / 10
Mean=11.1

Median -Arrange in Ascending order and find the middle value 

Median = 9+11/2 = 10

difference=Mean - Median
difference = 11.1 - 10
difference=1.1
